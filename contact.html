Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services, orders, and delivery.

How far in advance should I place a catering order?

For small orders (under 50 items), we recommend at least 2-3 days notice. For larger events or custom cakes, please give us at least 1-2 weeks advance notice to ensure we can accommodate your request perfectly.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer delivery within a 20-mile radius of our bakery. Delivery fees vary based on distance and order size. For catering orders over $200, delivery is free within 10 miles.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

We offer vegetarian options and can accommodate some dietary restrictions with advance notice. Please let us know about any allergies or dietary requirements when placing your order, and we'll do our best to help.

What is your cancellation policy?

For standard orders, cancellations made 24 hours before pickup/delivery receive a full refund. Custom cakes and large catering orders require 48-hour notice. A 50% deposit is required for orders over $100.

Do you provide setup for catering events?

Our Premium catering package includes on-site setup and service. For Basic and Standard packages, we deliver items ready to serve. Full event service can be added to any package for an additional fee.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and bank transfers. For catering orders, we require a 50% deposit to confirm your booking, with the balance due on delivery or pickup.
