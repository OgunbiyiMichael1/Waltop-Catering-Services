Our Story

From a small kitchen dream to becoming your favorite catering service, discover the passion behind every bite.

Our Beginning

A Recipe Born from Passion

Waltop Catering Services started as a small business in Ogun State, Nigeria, with a dream to share our local flavors with the world. Today, we are proud to serve a wide range of Nigerian snacks loved by many.

Today, we've grown from that small home kitchen to a fully-equipped bakery, but our commitment to quality remains unchanged. Every pie, every cake, and every pastry is still made with the same love and attention to detail that started it all.

We believe that great food brings people together. That's why we pour our hearts into creating snacks and treats that make your gatherings special, your celebrations memorable, and your everyday moments a little sweeter.
